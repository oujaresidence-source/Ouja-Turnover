# Ouja Studio v2 — Research Brief (short-form virality, 2025–2026)

Source: deep-research workflow (wf_d43f140d-9d1, 2026-07-08). 5 angles → 20 sources → 98
claims → 25 adversarially verified. Synthesis step aborted on a monthly spend limit, so
this brief is hand-synthesized from the verified + raw claims. **Verified = survived 2–3
independent refute votes against a primary/quoted source. Directional = from a single
secondary/blog source, not independently verified — use as guidance, never cite as fact.**

## The headline answer to the owner's complaint
Negative / drama framing is the WRONG strategy for a business brand account, and the
research backs the owner's instinct hard:

- **REFUTED (0–3):** "algorithms amplify rage regardless of sentiment / negative engagement
  counts the same as positive." This popular myth did not survive verification.
- **VERIFIED (TikTok primary):** negative viewer feedback ("Not interested") *actively
  suppresses* that content type in the feed. Negativity has a real algorithmic *downside*.
- **Convergent (many secondary sources):** rage/drama buys a short-term spike but damages
  brand equity, does not convert to loyalty or sales, and reads as inauthentic to modern
  audiences. Net-negative trade for a brand.
- **Nuance that matters:** a problem used as a *hook that resolves constructively* (tension →
  fix → delight) is fine and powerful. What's toxic is *unresolved* negativity, blame, or a
  story that leaves the brand looking bad. → This is the "hero arc" the v2 engine must enforce.

## Algorithm mechanics (VERIFIED — TikTok's own docs)
1. **Watch time / completion is the single most heavily weighted ranking factor**, above
   likes/shares/comments. (tiktok.com/transparency)
2. The system **predicts finish-vs-skip and watch duration**, then ranks by the combined
   prediction — so completion mechanics *directly* drive distribution.
3. **Finishing a longer video start-to-finish is a strong signal**, weighted above weak ones.
4. **Follower count and past viral track record are NOT direct ranking factors.** A single
   video can reach 500K–1M on its own engagement — the account's size doesn't gate it. (This
   is the answer to "make it stronger than me": one great video wins on its own merits.)
5. TikTok injects **feed diversity / similarity checks** — formulaic, repetitive formats get a
   built-in distribution penalty. Vary hooks and structures.
6. **Region personalization** structurally favors Saudi creators reaching Saudi audiences.
7. **Saves & shares are weighted above likes**; engineer story payoffs worth saving/sharing.

## Retention mechanics (mostly directional — treat as guidance)
- Hook must land in the **first 2–3 seconds**; proposition/message in the first 3s.
- **>65% retention at the 3s mark → ~4–7× impressions** (directional, uncited stat).
- **~70% completion = second-batch promotion threshold in 2026** (was ~50% in 2024; directional).
- **Pattern interrupt every ~4 seconds** keeps retention up vs a static talking head.
- **21–34s = highest completion (~62%)**; but total watch time also counts (a 40s @ 60% beats
  a 20s @ 70%). 30–60s is the story sweet spot; 60–90s can earn more reach IF retention holds.
- **Rewatch loops** (last line calls back to the hook) are an especially strong signal for
  sub-30s videos.

## Hooks — burned out vs working (2025–2026)
BURNED OUT (ban these): generic greetings ("hey guys, welcome back"), self-introduction opens,
vague bold statements ("you're doing X wrong"), bait-and-switch clickbait questions,
"you won't believe" / "wait till the end" / "shocking story", tired "POV:", cold "did you
know?", rage-bait / manufactured outrage, fabricated stats.

STILL WORKING (favor these): **Identity Call** ("if you own an apartment in Riyadh…" — highest
scoring), **Open Loop** (real curiosity gap from a true story), **Confession** (honest
admission — fits the transparency brand), **Contrarian Strike** (grounded contrarian take, not
rage), **Proof-first** (lead with real numbers/results), **specific-pain-point question**
(+~23% retention vs generic open). Specificity + one real number + one sensory detail = instant
credibility.

## Saudi / Gulf specifics
- ~22M active KSA users, ~95 min/day (highest in MENA, ~30% above global).
- **Arabic earns 2–3× engagement vs English; Gulf dialect > MSA** (Najdi is right; MSA reads
  as formal/disconnected).
- **Behind-the-scenes = 5–8% engagement for Saudi business accounts** (a top format).
- **Real estate + F&B have the highest TikTok ROI in KSA**; only ~35% of KSA businesses market
  on TikTok (low competition = opportunity).
- Best length: 30–60s overall; <15s for pure hook-style; 1–3 min for educational.
- **>30% of TikTokers watch on mute** → on-screen text is mandatory, not optional.
- Spoken words are indexed for search → say the city / niche / apartment type aloud.
- MENA trend signals (TikTok "What's Next"): **Curiosity Peaked, Storytelling Unhinged,
  Bridging the Trust Gap.** GM advice: "creative bravery" + community over corporate polish.
- **79% of users prefer TikTok-native content** over repurposed/polished corporate; UGC-style
  gets up to **2.4× engagement**.

## Winning archetypes for STR / hospitality founder-creators (all POSITIVE, brand-forward)
Real creators/cases, though most gave no absolute view counts:
1. **Transformation / before-after** — raw unit → premium stay (Zoey Berghoff; Airbnb's top
   engagement was "wow-space tours").
2. **Financial transparency / real numbers** — per-booking earnings, costs, occupancy (Monica
   Robles @monicas.house; @inayahmcmillan). *Directly fits Faisal's transparency brand.*
3. **Day-in-the-life / behind-the-scenes** of running the operation (Gathern hosts; Monica
   Robles).
4. **Candid founder journey** — wins AND honest struggles, told with dignity, not as drama
   (@thekarwells).
5. **Guest-experience / hospitality showcase** — the care and the save (Gathern).
6. **Hero-save** — something went sideways, the team fixed it fast, the guest left amazed.
   (This is how a "problem" conversation becomes POSITIVE brand content.)
7. **Weird/heartwarming real moment** — a genuinely delightful or surprising true request.

## Design implications (fed into the v2 spec)
- Editorial lens flips from "find drama/tension" to "find proof Ouja does exceptional work."
- A **brand-impact gate**: any story that would leave Ouja looking bad, or can only be told as
  unresolved negativity, is blocked from becoming a content idea.
- Problems are allowed ONLY as hero-arc setup that resolves into competence/care.
- Every idea card must carry a **"why this will work"** line tied to a specific mechanic above.
- Hooks are generated from the working-list and validated against the burned-out ban-list.
- Scan the last ~2000 real conversations; run daily; push a daily Discord digest.
